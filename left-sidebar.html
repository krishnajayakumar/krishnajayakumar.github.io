REST API

  • Infermedica

    Infermedica provides an API for patient triage and preliminary medical diagnosis that can help to implement an intelligent symptom checker or an adaptive patient intake form for the health application. If we send a patient’s health data (such as symptoms, risk factors, lab tests results or demographics), the AI inference engine will analyze the data and provide us with a list of likely conditions and relevant observations to verify. This is possible thanks to sophisticated statistical algorithms that use to perform diagnostic reasoning.

  • Firebase Management API

    The Firebase Management API enables programmatic setup and management of Firebase projects, including a project's Firebase resources and Firebase apps.

  • Facebook SDK

    The Facebook SDK for Android enables people to sign into your app with Facebook Login. When people log into your app with Facebook they can grant permissions to your app so you can retrieve information or perform actions on Facebook on their behalf.
